Machines

Singer Sewing Machine

Easy threading, domestic use. Always use correct thread and needle for fabric type.

Double Needle Machine

Sews two parallel rows. Maintain proper thread tension, ideal for denim and shirts.

Overlock Machine

Used for finishing seams. Clean blades regularly and thread in order.

Flatlock Machine

Best for knits and sportswear. Keep feed and tension balanced for neat finish.

Embroidery Machine

Digitally programmed to stitch designs. Requires stabilizers and hooping for accuracy.

Power Loom

Automated weaving machine. Ensure shuttle movement is smooth and warp is tight.

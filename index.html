The garment industry in India is a large and diverse sector, encompassing everything from raw material production to the export of ready-made garments. It generates significant revenue and employment and is driven by both large corporations and small manufacturers.

Order System

A Purchase Order (PO) is a legal document between a buyer and a garment manufacturer. It includes garment style, quantity, delivery, pricing, and payment details.
